About Us

Kesari Tours Pvt. Ltd. strongly believes and follows the concept of Kaizen "change for the better", refers to philosophy of continuous improvement of processes which will provide maximum satisfaction to our Guest. And keep us flexible in dynamic environment to serve them better. Kesari believes in providing innovative holiday ideas to its guests. It has hence created unique concepts to cater to travellers' ever-evolving needs, which have been acknowledged as innovations in the Indian Tourism Industry. Naturally, they have also translated into wide smiles on the faces of happy guests! Kesari Tours Pvt. Ltd. headquartered in Mumbai, is a World Class Travel Company, ISO 9001:2008 & BS OHSAS 18001:2007 Certified with a penchant for making every guest's travel experience a lifetime memory. Ever since its inception in 1984, its trailblazing journey began with a pledge: ' When you make a promise to your guests, honour it.' Through our constant dedication to customer satisfaction and unwavering commitment to excellence, we have earned our stripes in this competitive travel industry. Over the past 30 years, we have grown from strength to strength with our whole Kesari family across all departments, working towards excellence through innovation in the tourism industry Today, Kesari has become a household name for travellers across the length and breadth of our country. Kesari has a substantial portfolio of domestic and international holiday destinations to its credit, spreading across the seven continents.
